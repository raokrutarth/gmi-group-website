About Us

Prime Aluminum Supplier in Dubai, UAE

We specialise in manufacturing high grade aluminium casting alloys, usually low Iron secondary based. Our high integrity alloys are used in a wide range of applications, including aerospace and automotive, and are produced to all major International standards including LM6, LM3, A360, ADC12, A3105 and etc. ISO 3522:2007 chemical composition alloys available upon request.

Our manufacturing team has 20 years of experience and strongly committed to providing the highest quality products to establish long term relationships.
